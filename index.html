🎓 About This Model

1

Problem: Traditional banks only check PD. If PD > 10%, they reject — ignoring how profitable the customer actually is.

2

Model: Logistic Regression + Gradient Boosting predicts PD. Then Expected Loss, Net Profit, Economic Capital, and RAROC are derived.

3

Key insight: RAROC ≥ Hurdle Rate → Approve, even if PD seems high. Interest income must justify the risk taken.

4

Result: RAROC model approved 39.5% more customers, unlocking ₹159 Lakhs of additional profit while controlling portfolio risk.
