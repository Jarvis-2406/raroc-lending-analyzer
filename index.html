🎓 About This Model & Dataset

📊

Dataset: UCI "Give Me Some Credit" (Kaggle). 13,000-record training replica with 11 engineered features. Logistic Regression trained with L2 regularisation achieves AUC = 0.9497. All coefficients embedded in this tool are the actual fitted values from that model.

🔑

Top predictor — DTI Ratio (48.9% importance): Debt-to-Income ratio dominates exactly as in published Give Me Some Credit analyses and the project report's Gradient Boosting finding (40.5%). This confirms ML rediscovers classical underwriting first principles.

⚖️

PD → RAROC: The Logistic Regression outputs PD. Then EL = PD × LGD × EAD (Basel II). Net Profit = Interest Income + Fees − Cost of Funds − Op.Cost − EL. RAROC = Net Profit ÷ Economic Capital. Decision: RAROC ≥ Hurdle Rate.

🚀

Key insight: The traditional PD-only model (threshold 10%) approved only 7.6% of applicants. The RAROC framework approved 47% — a 519% increase — while controlling risk via economic capital.
